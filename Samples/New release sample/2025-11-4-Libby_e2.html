New pattern release: Libby princess seams raglan knit top

Meet our new pattern Libby – a knit pullover with raglan sleeves and princess seams that add gentle shaping and beautiful seam lines. Libby is drafted for knit fabrics and works well for everyday wear, from casual layers to slightly warmer tops in cooler weather. It is designed to allow a light layer underneath.

The princess seam panels offer simple color blocking options, and you can choose from four necklines (regular crew, lower crew, cowl, and turtleneck), two hem lengths, and multiple sleeve finishes. Sew it in French terry, sweater knits, or cotton jersey to suit your wardrobe.

Libby is a quick and satisfying sew.

The pattern has the following options: 

- Semi fitted at the shoulders and bust, relaxed at the waist and hips pullover
- Full bust pieces for 4+ inches difference between upper and full bust
- Raglan sleeves
- Princess seams at the front and back
- Neckline options: regular crew, lower crew, soft cowl neck or turtleneck
- Sleeve options: 3/4 hemmed, long hemmed or finished with cuffs
- Hem options: hip or lower hip length
- Available in Petite / Regular / Tall sizes, from US0 to US30
- Available in A4 / US Letter / A0 and Projector formats
- Difficulty level: 1-2 out of 10
- Most testers completed the top in 1-3 hours (including cutting and sewing)
- A great addition to your pattern library for everyday basics
